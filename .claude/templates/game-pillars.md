## Pillar Validation Checklist

For every proposed feature, answer:

- [ ] Does it support at least one pillar?
- [ ] Does it contradict any pillar?
- [ ] If it's pillar-neutral, is it truly necessary?
- [ ] Could it be simplified to better align with pillars?
